who we are

A lifelong pull toward animals

I'm Sam, and animals have been part of my life for as long as I can remember. When I was three, I somehow ended up curled inside a kennel with a litter of puppies — one asleep in my lap. The connection was obvious, and she became mine. I named her Chuppa Chup. That's us in the photo a few years later — but my love for animals started that day.

Horses took over my world not long after. I was the kid who memorised every breed, ran around the playground like a horse, and did anything I could just to be near them. That slightly crazy horse‑girl obsession shaped so much of who I became.

As I grew up, animals naturally became the centre of my everyday life — fostering, adopting, showing, grooming, walking, and caring for them in just about every way imaginable. Through mobile dog grooming and fostering I worked hands-on with dogs of every breed and temperament, learning to handle the nervous ones, the excitable ones, and the strong-willed ones with equal patience and calm. I also ended up owning and competing horses for most of my life, which gave me a deep understanding of their needs, quirks, and the trust it takes to truly care for them. That lifetime with animals has taught me to meet every personality with confidence — and to let their true selves shine.

What matters most

Every animal has their own way of moving through the world. My approach is always to meet them where they are and care for them in the way that suits them best.

At the end of the day, the thing that matters most to me is that every animal in my care feels safe, understood, and genuinely happy. That's the heart behind The Happy Pack — a little community built on trust, comfort, and the joy animals bring into our lives.

good to know

A few important things

☀️
Hot weather walks

If it is too warm to walk safely, we will shorten the walk and spend the remaining time somewhere cool and comfortable at home. Your dog's wellbeing always comes first.

🌧️
Rain? No problem

A bit of rain won't stop a walk — we and your dog will handle it together. In the case of lightning or storms, we will always attempt to reschedule or offer a full refund.

📅
Cancellations

24 hours notice is appreciated for cancellations so we can manage bookings fairly for everyone.

💉
Vaccinations required

All pets must be up to date with their vaccinations. As we visit multiple animals each day, this keeps every pet in our care safe and healthy.

🚗
Service area & travel

Based in Indooroopilly, servicing surrounding suburbs. A travel fee may apply for locations outside the standard service area — just ask.

🐕
Behaviour on walks

Generally, a few little quirks are totally fine — as long as dogs are safe and non-reactive on lead. This keeps every walk enjoyable for everyone.

🏷️ Extended stay discount — bookings of a week or more receive 10% off. Just mention it when you get in touch.
